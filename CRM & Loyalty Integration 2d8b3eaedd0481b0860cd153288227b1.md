# CRM & Loyalty Integration

Domain: Loyalty

| Project description | The CRM & Loyalty Integration project is a comprehensive solution developed to unify customer relationship management and loyalty program operations. Replacing a legacy system, it integrates **Salesforce CRM** with **SFCC** and **EPOS**, enabling real-time synchronization of customer profiles, transactions, and rewards. The platform supports key loyalty functionalities, including membership tiering and voucher redemption, while also connecting to **Emarsys** for automated marketing communications. This integration empowers businesses to deliver personalized experiences, improve customer retention, and increase engagement through data-driven insights and automation. |
