# SFCC Checkout Optimization

Featured: Yes
Problem: Customers faced high cart abandonment due to slow checkout and payment failures.
Tag: Project
Domain: E-commerce

| Project description | The SFCC Checkout Optimization project is an initiative aimed at enhancing the performance and user experience of the online checkout process. Designed to address issues of cart abandonment and payment failures, the system revamps the checkout flow on Salesforce Commerce Cloud by introducing streamlined UI/UX improvements and secure payment options. It integrates major payment gateways such as **Stripe** and **PayPal**, supporting **3D Secure authentication** to ensure transaction safety and compliance. This solution enables faster, more reliable transactions, ultimately driving higher conversion rates and customer satisfaction |
